# YJunction
Y Junction is a production-oriented service marketplace platform built with ASP.NET Core 8 and Clean Architecture, connecting customers with skilled professionals through a simple, service-first booking experience.

## Project Overview

Y Junction is a production-oriented service marketplace platform that connects customers with skilled workers and service professionals through a simple, service-first booking experience.

The project is being built as a backend-focused ASP.NET Core 8 application following Clean Architecture and modern software engineering practices.

---

## Vision

To build a scalable platform where customers can easily book trusted services while enabling skilled professionals to receive verified work opportunities.

The architecture is designed to support future expansion into multiple service industries without major redesign.

---

## Business Problem

Finding reliable service professionals is often difficult, time-consuming, and unorganized.

Customers usually don't know:

- Which professional they need
- Who is trustworthy
- How much the work should cost

Workers also struggle to receive consistent job opportunities.

---

## Solution

Y Junction simplifies the booking experience by allowing customers to select the required service instead of searching for workers.

The platform identifies suitable professionals and manages the booking process.
